Social media APP

Project Date Feb 1st, 2024
FrameWork Flutter
BackEnd Firebase

The Social Media App is designed to create a vibrant platform for users to share photos, videos, and stories, connect with friends, discover new content, and express themselves creatively. Similar to Instagram, it offers a visually rich experience, fostering a global community of users who interact through likes, comments, and messages.

Users can easily register via email, phone number, or social media accounts and create customizable profiles with bios and profile pictures. The app allows users to upload photos and videos, apply filters, and edit content. Temporary stories that disappear after 24 hours and short video clips known as reels add to the dynamic content creation features.

The explore page provides personalized content recommendations based on user interests and activity. Users can tag friends, use hashtags, like, and comment on posts to engage with others. Private messaging is available for one-on-one or group conversations, enhancing social interaction.

The app supports monetization through sponsored posts, in-app purchases, and subscription options, providing revenue opportunities for both users and businesses. Privacy features include account settings for profile visibility, content control options, and robust data security practices.
