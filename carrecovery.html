Car Recovery

Project Date March 1th, 2021
FrameWork PHP
BackEnd MySQL

The Car Recovery Website is designed to provide efficient and reliable roadside assistance and vehicle recovery services for drivers in need. This platform connects users with a network of professional recovery operators, ensuring prompt help in case of breakdowns, accidents, or other vehicle-related issues.

Users can easily access the website to request assistance by entering their location and details of their vehicle problem. The site features an intuitive interface, making it simple to navigate and request services quickly. Upon submitting a request, users receive immediate confirmation and estimated arrival times of recovery operators.

The website offers a comprehensive range of services, including towing, jump-starts, tire changes, fuel delivery, and lockout assistance. Each service is described in detail, providing users with a clear understanding of what to expect.

Professional recovery operators can register on the platform, creating profiles that include their certifications, service areas, and customer reviews. This transparency helps users make informed decisions when selecting a service provider.

To enhance user experience, the website includes real-time tracking of the recovery vehicle, allowing users to monitor the progress of their assistance. Additionally, the platform provides 24/7 customer support through live chat and phone, ensuring that help is always available when needed.
